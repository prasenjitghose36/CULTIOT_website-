About us

Farmers, customarily being the epitome of agony, whose importance being shunned by the vicious generations in the flow of time and scuffling below the iniquity of power possessors lesser brains, they seek only one thing 'HELP'. Our team looked forward into the future and by realising their superlative significance, being staggered, came forward to protect the base of farming by reshaping it using advanced technologies like IOT, cloud computing and machine learning...etc. So come with us to help them who feed us...

We are a small team from Veer Surendra Sai University of Technology who wish to make life of farmers better by using the advanced iot solutions and cloud computing solution we want to use our technical knowlegde to help the farmers as much as possible hence creating a difference.
